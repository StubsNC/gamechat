Mission Statement

At GameChat, our mission is to bring players together and enhance their gaming experience by providing an easy and convenient way to share game clips and stats with friends. We believe in the power of community and competition, and strive to create a fun and engaging platform for players to connect and challenge each other. Through our app, we aim to facilitate meaningful connections and friendly rivalry among gamers, fostering a sense of camaraderie and support. Whether you're looking to show off your skills or just keep in touch with your gaming crew, GameChat is here to make it happen.

Can I track my stats and compete with my friends in GameChat?

Yes, GameChat allows you to track and compare your stats with your friends. The app will automatically gather data on your gameplay, including headshots and other key metrics. You can view your own stats at any time, and compare them with your friends' stats to see who comes out on top. You can also participate in friendly competitions and challenges within the app to test your skills against your friends.

Is GameChat free to use?
GameChat is a free service, and you can use all of its features at no cost. However, the app does offer optional in-app purchases and premium subscriptions that unlock additional features and benefits. These purchases are completely optional and are not necessary to use the app.
